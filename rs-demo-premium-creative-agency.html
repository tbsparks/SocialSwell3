Why do Reviews Matter?

Reviews mean everything to your business, seriously Use Swell to:

Improve Local SEO (search ranking)

Having a consistent flow of positive reviews across multiple sites helps push your business to the top of local searches. Reviews are one of the most effective ways to get ahead of your local competition.

Increase Customer Loyalty

Customers feel more attached to a business when they talk about it with their friends. Making it easy for them to give you feedback or share a review is the first step in building a loyal customer base.

This one is simple. More reviews means more customers meens more revenue. Use reviews as an easy way to climb to the top of search rankings and spread your social reach to attract new customers looking for the product or services you offer.
